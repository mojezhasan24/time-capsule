## 🎬 **Animation Library**

### **Keyframe Animations:**
1. **shimmer** - Gradient text shine (3s infinite)
2. **gradientShift** - Button background shift (3s infinite)
3. **btnShine** - Light sweep across buttons (3s infinite)
4. **twinkle** - Star opacity pulse (5s infinite)
5. **nebulaPulse** - Background scale/breath (10s infinite)
6. **float** - Object rotation/drift (20s infinite)
7. **particleFade** - Particle lifecycle (1s)
8. **fadeIn** - Content reveal (0.8-1s)
9. **glitch-1/2** - Text distortion (0.3s infinite)

### **Transition Timing:**
- **Standard**: cubic-bezier(0.175, 0.885, 0.32, 1.275)
- **Smooth**: ease-out, ease-in-out
- **Bouncy**: cubic-bezier(0.68, -0.55, 0.265, 1.55)
